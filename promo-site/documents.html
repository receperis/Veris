How to Use Veris

Follow this guide to get the most out of your new translation and vocabulary-building tool.

1. Instant Translation

Getting a translation is effortless. Simply use your mouse to highlight any word or piece of text on a webpage. As soon as you release the mouse button, a small, non-intrusive popup will appear, showing the translation in your preferred language.

2. Saving Words to Your Vocabulary

When you translate a word or phrase you want to remember, click the "Save" or bookmark icon within the translation popup. This action adds the term and its translation to your personal vocabulary list, which you can access anytime from the extension's main panel.

3. Practicing with Spaced Repetition (SRS)

To truly learn your saved words, navigate to the "Practice" or "SRS" tab in the extension. Our Spaced Repetition System will present you with flashcards at optimal intervals, helping you commit words to your long-term memory. The more you practice, the smarter the algorithm gets.

4. Tracking Your Learning Progress

Visit the "Stats" or "Dashboard" section to see detailed analytics about your learning journey. You can track how many words you've saved, your practice consistency, and your overall progress over time. This feedback helps you stay motivated and focused on your goals.
